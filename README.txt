Instructions:
Set up the game in multiplayer deathmatch.
Weapons have been moded in function and appearance, and abilities and leveling has been added.

Levels: you gain 1 level per kill.

Extra Jumps: earn 1 extra jump per level. press space after jumping in an attempt to jump again.
Health Regen: Regen health if lower than 100 percent. Press Q. 

Ammo Regen: Regen Bullets and Shells, no limit. Higher level = more bullets. Press J.
I have included a config file.
